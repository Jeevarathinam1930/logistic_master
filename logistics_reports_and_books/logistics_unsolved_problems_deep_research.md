## Executive Takeaway

The largest unsolved logistics opportunities are not single-mode routing problems. They sit where many parties must coordinate but do not share clean, timely, trusted data: multi-tier visibility, carrier/port/warehouse handoffs, inventory and demand distortion, customs/tariff compliance, demurrage and detention, and exception management. Most existing ERP, TMS, WMS, visibility, planning, and control-tower tools claim to solve pieces of these problems, but recurring evidence from industry reports, regulators, user reviews, and practitioner forums shows the gaps are still material.

The strongest startup white spaces are: cross-company logistics data fabric, demurrage/detention prevention, AI-native customs and tariff compliance, warehouse readiness prediction, dock detention elimination, freight fraud prevention, cold-chain excursion attribution, returns disposition optimization, exception-resolution agents, and small/mid-market integration layers for companies trapped between spreadsheets and enterprise systems.

## Scoring Method

All scores are 1-10. Final Score = Impact x Frequency x Market Opportunity x Current Solution Gap.

- Impact: financial loss, delay, risk, service failure, or regulatory exposure.
- Frequency: how often the issue appears across modes, companies, or shipments.
- Market Opportunity: willingness to pay, market size, and budget owner clarity.
- Current Solution Gap: how incomplete current tools are in real operations.
- Solvability: separate directional estimate used in startup analysis, not part of final score.
